Buffalo Wings

Baked buffalo wings. Dredged with flour, cayenne pepper, garlic powder, and salt. Sauce made with butter and Frank's Red Hot (or a sauce of your choice).

Ingredients

  • cooking spray
  • 3/4 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 20 chicken wings
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 1/2 cup Frank's Red Hot (or hot sauce of your choice
  • 4 eggs (optional)

Directions

  1. Line baking sheet with aluminum foil, and lightly grease with cooking spray.
  2. Place flour, cayenne pepper, garlic powder, and salt into a resealable bag, and shake to mix
  3. Coat the wings in egg yolk (optional). Add the wings to the bag, seal it, and shake until wings are thoroughly coated.
  4. Transfer wings onto baking sheet and refrigerate for at least an hour.
  5. Preheat oven to 450. Whisk together melted butter and hot sauce.
  6. Bake the wings for 45 minutes, flipping the wings half way through.
  7. Coat wings in hot sauce and serve.
