Pepperoni Pizza

A simple pepperoni pizza that can be baked at home.

Can be baked in the oven without a pizza stone.

Ingredients

Pizza Sauce

  • 1/2 (12ounce) can of tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano, crushed
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil, crushed
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Pizza Dough

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour, or more as needed
  • ).50 ounce of instant yeast
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar
  • 1.5 teaspoons of salt
  • 1 and 1/3 cups of warm water (120F to 130F)
  • 1/3 cup of oil

Toppings

  • 6 ounces pepperoni, or more if preferred
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese, or more if preferred

Directions

  1. Combine all sauce ingredients with 1/2 cup of room temperature water in a medium size bowl.
  2. Combine 2 cups of flour with yeast, sugar, and salt. Add the warm water and oil and mix until blended. Gradually add flour until a soft, stick ball of dough is formed.
  3. Knead on a floured surface until dough is smooth and elastic, adding more flour if needed. Cover the dough and let rest for 10 - 30 minutes.
  4. Divide dough in half. Pat dough into 12-inch greased pizza pan.
  5. Preheat oven to 425F. Top crust with sauce, pepperoni, and cheese.
  6. Bake for 20 minutes or until crust is golden brown and cheese is bubbly.
