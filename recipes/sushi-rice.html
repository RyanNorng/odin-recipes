Sushi Rice

Sushi rice, made with sugar and vinegar.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups uncooked white rice
  • 3 cups water
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup rice vinegar
  • 1 and 1/4 teaspoons salt

Directions

  1. Mix together the rice vinegar, sugar, and salt until dissolved in a small bowl.
  2. Rinse the rice.
  3. Drain the rice and add it into a saucepan with 3 cups of water.
  4. Cover and bring rice to a boil over high heat, then reduce heat to low and simmer for 15 minutes. Remove from heat and allow to stand, covered, for 5 minutes.
  5. Transfer rice into a bowl. Stir in vinegar mixture until well incorporated. Allow to cool at room temperature.
